Indoor Heat Deaths

Over the summers of 2016 through 2018, more than one hundred people in Maricopa County, Arizona were found dead indoors from extreme heat.

James Allen Dickinson, 63, was found dead in his home in the summer of 2017. He was last known alive two hours earlier. His air conditioning had broken the day before, and it was about 100 degrees inside. The medical examiner said environmental heat contributed to his death.
